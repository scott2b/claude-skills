## Critical Reminder

**KEY POINT**: The more intricate the opponent's plan, and the better it's hidden, the better the plot will be.

This is the most important and most often missing beat in Crime stories. Most writers focus only on the hero's plan and forget that the opponent also has a detailed strategy.

## The Joker Standard

Compare to The Joker in *The Dark Knight* (one of cinema's greatest opponent plans):

**The Joker's Plan**:
- Series of moral conundrums designed to expose true nature of humanity
- Each attack is a philosophical test
- Massive Prisoner's Dilemma with two ships
- Dark Philosopher who constructs criminal plots expressing moral vision
- Master schemer whose intellect shown through ability to plot
- Acts not for greed or revenge but for the game
